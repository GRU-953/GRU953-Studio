## Free — no commercial licence needed

You are already covered by the free licence, at no cost, if your use is
**non-commercial**. That includes:

- **Personal use** — your own projects, learning, experimenting, and hobbies.
- **Research and study** — for the benefit of public knowledge.
- **Charities, schools, universities, and public-benefit organisations** —
  including government, public health, public safety, and environmental bodies —
  regardless of how they are funded.

If that's you, there is nothing to buy, sign, or ask for. Enjoy it.

---

## When you need a commercial licence

You need a separate, paid commercial licence if you use GRU953-Studio — or
anything you build with it — to make money or as part of a commercial offering.
For example:

- **Selling an app you built with GRU953-Studio**, or charging people to use it.
- **Using it inside a business** to build software that the business runs on or
  sells.
- **Offering it (or a version of it) as part of a paid product or service.**
- **Bundling or redistributing it** as part of something commercial.

In short: if there's a commercial or revenue-generating purpose behind the use,
the free non-commercial licence does not cover it — a commercial licence does.

> **Not sure which side of the line you're on?** Just ask. A one-line email
> describing what you'd like to do is enough, and there's no obligation.
