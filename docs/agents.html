The full team

38 specialist roles, in plain English

You only ever talk to the Project Lead — this page exists purely for the curious. Every project wakes only the specialists it actually needs; a tiny website wakes a handful, a bigger app with money, data and content wakes more.

Core team — most projects use these

Project LeadOpus
Your single point of contact. Runs the whole build from start to finish, decides how big a job your project is, brings in the right specialists, and merges everything into one plain-English reply.
InterviewerSonnet
Writes the pop-up multiple-choice questions the Project Lead shows you — always a clear question with a recommended answer, never a wall of prose.
ArchitectOpus
Proposes 2–3 sensible ways to build your app for your chosen platform, then breaks the confirmed plan into small, checkable steps in the right order.
Scope GuardianSonnet
Stops unrequested extras quietly creeping in, and keeps a permanent record of anything deliberately left out, so a cut idea is never silently reintroduced.
BuilderSonnet
Writes the code, one small step at a time. On bigger projects, two work in parallel without ever interfering with each other. Hands platform-specific work to the matching language specialist.
ReviewerOpus
An independent pair of eyes on every piece of code — checks it's correct first, then trims anything that shouldn't have been built.
TesterSonnet
Decides what "tested enough" means for your project, then proves it by actually running the checks — never claims something works without running it.
Security & Compliance AuditorSonnet
Scans for leaked passwords, known weaknesses, and licence problems before anything ships — a mandatory check on every single project, however small.
Brand GuardianHaiku
Keeps your app's look, name and tone consistent with whatever visual identity you've chosen.
AI DeveloperSonnet
The one owner of any AI feature your app needs — writes the prompt, wires it up safely, and builds in "say I don't know" rather than letting it guess.
FixerSonnet
The smallest, most precise repair when something breaks — quietly tries a couple of fixes first, and only interrupts you if that doesn't work.
Cost MonitorHaiku
Keeps spending on the cheaper side by default, and shows you a plain estimate before anything unusually expensive.
PublisherSonnet
Decides the version number, writes honest release notes, and ships your finished project — privately first, under your own GitHub name, with a separate step to make it public.
Memory KeeperHaiku
Remembers everything about your project between sessions, in plain-text notes, so nothing is ever lost if you close the computer.

Content team — joins in once you approve the mock-up

Content DirectorSonnet
Plans your app's real content — text, images, audio, video — from the approved design, and decides (with you) whether generated media is switched on.
Text Content SpecialistSonnet
Writes your app's actual buttons, onboarding and messages, natively in Bangla and English.
Image Content SpecialistSonnet
Generates icons and illustrations with your own Google account, in the right sizes for every platform, always with alt-text for accessibility.
Audio Content SpecialistSonnet
Generates narration, speech and sound, with a transcript provided for every clip.
Video Content SpecialistSonnet
Generates short video clips and walkthroughs, with captions provided for every one.

Brought in only when your project needs them

Maintenance AgentSonnet
Picks up an already-published project safely when you come back to change or grow it, rather than starting over.
DevOps EngineerSonnet
Handles hosting and putting your app online, plus standard self-recovery so a live app quietly restarts itself if it ever crashes.
Responsible-AI ReviewerOpus
An independent fairness and honesty check for any AI feature that meaningfully affects a real decision about a person.
UX DesignerSonnet
Makes the app's flow and wording pleasant and easy to follow.
Accessibility SpecialistSonnet
Makes sure the app is genuinely usable by people with disabilities.
Technical WriterSonnet
Writes a plain-English guide for your finished app, so someone other than the builder can use it.
Data EngineerSonnet
Designs safe, sensible data storage for any app that keeps information beyond one session.
Localisation SpecialistSonnet
Makes an app work properly in more than one language, e.g. English and Bangla together.
ResearcherSonnet
Checks current facts the team needs to decide well, and can suggest an existing tool that already solves a task — always asking before installing anything.

Several core-team roles can also ask a free, local AI model (Ollama) for an independent second opinion on their own work — always your choice, and it always tells you the download size before anything installs.
