Picking up where you left off

Command centre

Pausing partway through

Say "pause this" any time, close your laptop, and come back whenever suits you — a plain dashboard shows exactly what's done, what's next, and what's waiting on you.

Maintenance Agent

Growing something already published

Just describe the change — "add a way to search my recipe app by ingredient" — and the Maintenance Agent picks it up safely, rather than starting over.

Claude Code on the web

Switching to the browser

Start on your laptop, continue from a browser tab on Claude Code on the web — with your consent, your planning notes travel with you.
