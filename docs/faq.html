FAQ

Questions people ask most

Straight, honest answers — if yours isn't here, the GitHub issues page is always open, no technical detail required.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. You describe your idea in plain words and answer simple pop-up questions. You never write or read a single line of code.

What does it cost?

GRU953-Studio itself is free (see the licence). You pay only for your existing Claude usage. Generating images, audio or video is entirely optional and uses your own Google account — the studio always shows you the estimated cost and asks before spending anything.

Is my data private?

Your project's planning notes stay in a plain-text folder on your own machine. Publishing goes to your own GitHub account, private by default — making it public is a separate step you choose. If you use Claude Code on the web, notes are only backed up to a private branch with your explicit yes.

Can it build an iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac or Linux app?

Yes — natively, for every one of those. See the use-case gallery for real examples on each platform, and the Agents directory for the specialist behind each language.

Does it work in languages other than English?

Your app's in-app text can be written natively in both Bangla and English. Support for other languages in the interview and interface can grow from there — ask, and the team will tell you honestly what's possible today.

I don't have a Google account for Gemini — is that a problem?

Not at all. Written content needs no Google account — it's produced by Claude directly. If you skip Gemini, the studio simply gives you a clear, step-by-step guide to add images, audio or video yourself.

Can I stop partway through and come back later?

Yes, any time — say "pause this," close your laptop, and return whenever suits you. Nothing is lost; see the Getting Started guide for the exact commands.

What happens if I run out of my daily Claude usage mid-build?

Nothing is lost. Progress is saved constantly; come back later and say "continue," or run /studio again — it resumes exactly where it stopped.

Can I see the app before it's actually built?

Yes — before any real code is written, you're shown a clickable mock-up of the screens plus a plain-English plan of what ships first. Nothing real is built until you approve both.

Can I sell an app I build with GRU953-Studio?

Yes, with a separate paid commercial licence — the free licence covers personal, learning and non-commercial use. See the "Commercial use" part of the licence, or email aninda.sh15@gmail.com.

How is my app kept safe before it's published?

Automatic checks scan for leaked passwords, known weaknesses, and licence problems before anything ships — a mandatory step on every project, however small. Full technical detail is openly documented in SECURITY.md.

What happens if something goes wrong while it's building?

It tries a couple of small fixes quietly on its own first. If that doesn't work, it tells you plainly what's blocking progress and what your choices are — it never leaves something silently broken. See Troubleshooting for common bumps.

Is this made or endorsed by Anthropic or Google?

No. GRU953-Studio is an independent, unofficial plugin for Claude Code, not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Anthropic (Claude's maker) or Google (Gemini's maker).

Does it work on Claude Code on the web, not just the desktop app?

Yes, both. On the web, where the workspace is temporary, it can — only with your yes — back your project's planning notes up to a private branch so you can carry on later.
