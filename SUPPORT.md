# Getting help

_GRU953-Studio_

Stuck, unsure, or just have a question? Here's where to look — start at the
top and work down. You never need any technical knowledge to ask for help.

### 1. The guide (most answers live here)
The **[GRU953-Studio Wiki](https://github.com/GRU-953/GRU953-Studio/wiki)** is
the full, plain-English handbook: how to install it, how to connect Ollama and
Gemini, what every part does, sample projects to try, a
**[Troubleshooting](https://github.com/GRU-953/GRU953-Studio/wiki/Troubleshooting)**
page, and an **[FAQ](https://github.com/GRU-953/GRU953-Studio/wiki/FAQ)**.

### 2. Ask a question
For a "how do I…?" or "is it meant to do this?" question, open a friendly
thread in **[Discussions](https://github.com/GRU-953/GRU953-Studio/discussions)**.
No technical detail required — describe it however feels natural.

### 3. Report a problem or suggest an idea
If something looks broken, or you'd like a new feature, open an
**[Issue](https://github.com/GRU-953/GRU953-Studio/issues/new/choose)**. There
are simple, guided forms — just fill in the plain-English boxes.

### 4. Security concerns
Please **don't** post a security problem in public. Follow the private steps in
the **[Security policy](SECURITY.md)** instead.

### 5. Commercial licensing
To use GRU953-Studio commercially, see
**[COMMERCIAL-LICENCE.md](COMMERCIAL-LICENCE.md)** or email
**aninda.sh15@gmail.com**.

---

A reminder that often helps: GRU953-Studio saves your progress constantly, so if
something goes wrong mid-project, **nothing is lost** — you can almost always
just come back and say "continue".
