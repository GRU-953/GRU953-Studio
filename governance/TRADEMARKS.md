The word mark **GRU953**, the **Soaring Bird** logo, and any lockup

Marks are *not* covered by the GRU953 Community Licence — copying

work as GRU953's.

## Always allowed (no permission needed)

- **Referential use.** Naming GRU953-Studio in text to truthfully refer to
  the project, its releases, or its assets ("built with GRU953-Studio").
- **Unmodified logo for reference.** Showing the unmodified logo to
  identify or link to GRU953-Studio in articles, talks, or comparison
  tables.
- **Compatibility statements.** "Works with GRU953-Studio" — provided
  your own branding is dominant and no endorsement is implied.

## Never allowed without written permission

- Using the Marks (or confusingly similar marks) as or in your own
  product name, company name, domain, app icon, or social handle.
- Modifying the logo: recolouring outside the published builds,
  distorting, adding effects, or combining it with other marks.
- Implying sponsorship, endorsement, or official status.
- Using the Marks on merchandise or in advertising for paid products.

- The brand name is always **GRU953** — one word, uppercase, no hyphen,

- The product name is **GRU953-Studio** — one hyphen, both words

- The GitHub *account handle* is `GRU-953` (a platform requirement); it

- In file and repository names the lowercase token `gru953-studio` is a
