# GRU953 Logo & Visual Identity — Usage Policy

GRU953-Studio's documentation and code are licensed under the GRU953
Community Licence 1.0 (see `LICENSE` and `NOTICE`) — free for personal and
non-commercial use, with commercial use requiring a paid licence. **The
GRU953 mark itself — the Soaring Bird logo, the GRU953 wordmark, and any
lockup combining them — is carved out from that general permission and
governed by this policy instead.** This keeps the identity consistent
across every GRU953 product, and prevents the mark from being legally
altered or reused as someone else's identity.

## You may, without asking

- Reproduce the logo **unaltered**, in one of its supplied builds (colour,
  reversed/white, single-colour, black), to refer to or link to
  GRU953-Studio — e.g. in a blog post, a "built with GRU953" credit, a
  compatible/integration listing, or press coverage.
- Use it at any size at or above the minimum stated in the GRU953 brand
  guidebook (120 px / 25 mm for the horizontal lockup, 24 px for the mark
  alone).

## You may not, without written permission

- Alter the shape, proportions, or colours of the mark, or recolour it
  outside the approved builds — including stretching, squashing, rotating,
  skewing, adding shadows/outlines/glows/bevels, or re-typesetting the
  wordmark.
- Use the mark (or anything confusingly similar to it) as your own logo, or
  in a way that implies GRU953 endorses, sponsors, or is affiliated with a
  product or organisation it is not.
- Sell merchandise, templates, or products whose primary value is the mark
  itself.
- Combine the mark with other logos, slogans, or imagery in a way that
  suggests a joint venture or partnership.

## Everything else stays open

The name **GRU953** in plain text, the brand colours (as hex values, which
are not proprietary), the typefaces (under their own open licences), and
all other documentation and assets remain under the GRU953 Community
Licence 1.0 as stated in `NOTICE` — only the graphic mark itself is scoped
by this policy (see also `TRADEMARKS.md`).
